Yoshiyuki Okuyama (January 23, 1991-) is a Japanese photographer born and raised in Tokyo, Japan.
He started to work with photography in high school, and during his time as a student he worked for several magazines,
creating a unique style of photography with vivid emotions and large-scale swinging movements.
